New

A state of the art solution for the visually impaired to perceive their surrounding. It is based on conversion of vision to other stimuli like audio providing an audio picture. It features a camera connected to a raspberry pi attached to a wearable that provides an audio feedback, through an app installed in the mobile phone. It is helpful in searching objects, navigation, facial recognition etc. It also includes a live video telecast to family members to help the visually impaired
